Thrift Quest — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 14 June 2026  ·  Contact: thriftradaraustralia@gmail.com

Thrift Quest ("we", "us", the "app") helps resellers identify second-hand items and estimate their resale value. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and who we share it with.

Information we collect

Account information. If you create an account, we collect your email address (and, if you sign in with Google, the basic profile information Google provides). Authentication is handled by Supabase.

Your saved items ("Finds"). When you save an item, we store the information you create about it — the item name, category, brand, our estimated value, condition, status, any sold price you enter, and links to your marketplace listings. This is stored in our database (Supabase) and associated with your account.

Your book list. If you use the Books feature, the titles/authors you add are stored with your account so we can match them when you scan a shelf.

Photos you scan. When you scan an item or shelf, the photo is sent to our AI providers (see below) to identify the item and estimate its value. Photos you attach to a saved Find are stored only on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

Usage and diagnostic data. We may process limited technical data needed to operate the app (for example, scan counts used to enforce free-tier limits).

We do not sell your personal data.

How we use your information

  • To identify items and estimate resale value
  • To save and sync your Finds and book list to your account
  • To manage your subscription and free-tier scan limits
  • To operate, secure, and improve the app

Third-party services we share data with

To provide the app's features, data is processed by:

  • Supabase — account authentication and storage of your Finds and book list
  • Anthropic (Claude) — scanned photos are sent for item identification and value estimation
  • Google Cloud Vision — scanned photos are sent for reverse-image product matching and text reading
  • eBay — item search terms are sent to retrieve recent sold-price data
  • Google — sign-in (OAuth), if you choose Google login
  • RevenueCat and Google Play Billing — subscription management and purchases
  • Exchange-rate provider — to convert estimated values into your chosen currency

Each provider processes data under its own privacy policy. We share only what is necessary to provide the relevant feature.

Data retention and deletion

We keep your account, Finds, and book list until you delete them. You can:

  • Delete individual Finds in the app at any time, and
  • Delete your entire account and all associated data from Profile → Delete account. This permanently removes your account, Finds, and book list and cannot be undone.

Full instructions are on our Account & Data Deletion page. To request deletion another way, email thriftradaraustralia@gmail.com.

International data transfers

We operate from Australia, and our service providers (including Anthropic and Google) process data on servers located in the United States and other countries. If you use the app from the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or elsewhere, your information — including photos you scan — may be transferred to and processed in countries whose data-protection laws differ from your own. Where required, these transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards such as the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses, which our providers offer under their own data-processing terms.

Your privacy rights

Legal bases (EEA/UK — GDPR). Where the GDPR applies, we process your data on these bases:

  • Performance of a contract — to create your account, run scans, save your Finds and book list, and manage your subscription;
  • Legitimate interests — to operate, secure, and improve the app and enforce free-tier limits;
  • Consent — where we ask for it (you can withdraw it at any time);
  • Legal obligation — where we must keep certain records.

Your rights (EEA/UK). You have the right to access, correct, delete, port, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data, and to lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority. You can exercise most of these in-app (Profile → Delete account) or by emailing us.

California (CCPA/CPRA). We do not sell or share your personal information, and we do not use it for cross-context behavioural advertising. California residents have the right to know what we collect, to request deletion, and to not be discriminated against for exercising these rights.

To exercise any right, contact thriftradaraustralia@gmail.com. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

Children

Thrift Quest is intended for adults (18+) and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16 (or the minimum age required in your country). If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we take reasonable steps to safeguard your information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
