Thrift Quest — Account & Data Deletion

How to delete your Thrift Quest account and the data associated with it.

Delete your account in the app (instant)

  1. Open Thrift Quest and make sure you're signed in.
  2. Tap your profile (the avatar in the top-right of the home screen).
  3. Scroll to Delete account and confirm.

This permanently deletes your account immediately and cannot be undone.

Or request deletion by email

If you can't access the app, email thriftradaraustralia@gmail.com from the address associated with your account and ask us to delete it. We'll action the request within 30 days.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes, from our systems:

  • Your account and login (email / Google sign-in identity)
  • All of your saved Finds (item names, values, conditions, statuses, sold prices, listing links)
  • Your book list (titles and authors you added or imported)
  • Any subscription association held on our side

What is not stored by us

  • Photos you attach to a Find are kept only on your device — they are never uploaded to our servers, and are removed when you delete the app.
  • Photos sent for scanning are processed by our AI providers to return a result and are not retained by Thrift Quest.

Retention

Account deletion is immediate when done in-app. Routine backups, if any, are purged on our provider's standard cycle (within 30 days). We retain no copy of your Finds or book list after deletion.
